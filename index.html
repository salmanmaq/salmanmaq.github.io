Publications

I have published the following papers in computer vision and machine learning:

  • [2020] m2caiSeg: Semantic Segmentation of Laparoscopic Images using Convolutional Neural Networks on arXiv.
      My main contributions were annotation of the m2caiTool dataset, training and evaluation of various neural network architectures including GANs for synthetic data generation as well as capusle networks for semantic segmentation. This core content of the paper was completed in 2018, however, that was delayed due to administrative reasons. You can find the dataset here. I also wrote the complete paper.
  • [2019] Real-time fish detection in complex backgrounds using probabilistic background modelling in Ecological Informatics.
      My main contributions were the complete annotation of the Fish4Knowledge dataset, trying out different background subtraction algorithms inlcuding Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), as wel as writing the literature review and method section of the paper.
  • [2018] People Counting in Dense Crowd Images Using Sparse Head Detections in IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology (TCSVT).
      I worked on the entire deep learning (CNN) section of the paper, from selection and training of the neural networks to writing that part of the paper.
