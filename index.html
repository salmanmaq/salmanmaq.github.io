About Me

I am a graduate student of Robotics and AI at National University of Sciences and Technology. Right now, I am working on my thesis on deep learning methods for semantic segmentation with Dr. Hasan Sajid at the Computer Vision and Machine Learning (CVML) Lab. I am also partly associated with the TUKL-NUST R&D Centre at my institute, where I am working on fish detection and classification with Dr. Faisal Shafait and Dr. Ahmad Salman. My research interests include the development of Artificial General Intelligence and Medical Analytics.
